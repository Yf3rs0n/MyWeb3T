“What time is it?” T This -without a doubt- is one the most common questions in the English language, and that’s because knowing the correct time is important to keep an organized scheduled. By knowing how to tell the time, a person can make sure to catch the bus at the right time or to determine if he or she will be able to attend that special event that’s being planned without a problem. For children, learning “ what’s the time” is one their milestones as they grow up, it allows them to be more independent, and to be able to have more control over their lives.
